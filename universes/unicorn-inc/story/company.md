# Unicorn Inc.

> **Draft / skeleton.** Starting point — expect changes as we flesh out the universe with Rutger.

## The hook in one line

A cheerful cooperative of fabelwezens running the realm's most magical supply chain — pegasus wings, bottled dragon fire, mammoth fur, phoenix feathers — from a sun-drenched cloud-island workshop where every shipment leaves with a rainbow on the invoice.

## Setting

**Hemelhof** — a sky-island floating above the Glittering Sea, perpetually in golden hour. The Unicorn Inc. campus is a constellation of pastel-painted timber workshops, glass greenhouses (for phoenix-feather aviaries) and an enormous open-air showroom where customers come to be fitted for their first pair of pegasus wings. Logistics rely on cloud-skiff caravans, courier sprites, and the occasional dragon airlift. Good for demo drama.

## The company

- **Name:** Unicorn Inc. (officially "The Hemelhof Cooperative for Magical Goods, trading as Unicorn Inc.").
- **Founded:** in-world equivalent of "two generations ago." Cooperative ownership — 25 fabelwezen members, no single CEO. A rotating Sparkle Circle (3 members) handles decisions.
- **Size:** ~25 staff across Sourcing, Fitting & Fulfilment, Magical QC, Customer Joy, and Accounts.
- **Customers:** mix of B2B (academies, knight-orders, royal quartermasters, courier guilds, theatre troupes) and B2C (adventurers, hobbyist enchanters, kids saving up for their first wings).
- **Suppliers:** specialist artisans across the realm — pegasus aviaries, dragon-fire bottlers, mammoth herders, phoenix sanctuaries, kraken-ink divers, basilisk-mirror polishers.

## The hook

Every product at Unicorn Inc. is **Sparkle-Stamped**: signed off by a named fabelwezen on the Magical QC bench before it ships, and accompanied by a personal handwritten note. This is Unicorn Inc.'s equivalent of Bram Visser calibration-roasting every Bean Forge machine — except in glitter ink.

## Product lines (sketch — rewrite with Rutger)

- **Wings & Flight** — fitted pegasus wings (sized S/M/L/XL), cloud-skiff kits, gryphon saddles, sylph-spun parachutes.
- **Fire & Force** — bottled dragon fire (mild / medium / inferno grades), phoenix-feather torches, basilisk-glance hand mirrors (caution: petrification risk), djinn-in-a-jar (one wish, terms apply).
- **Fur & Form** — mammoth-fur cloaks, kitsune-tail capes, kelpie-mane rope, golem-stone armour kits.
- **Ink & Insight** — kraken ink (squid-grade and giant-grade), oracle quills, dryad-bark notebooks, naiad scrying bowls.
- **Services** — wing-fitting appointments, fire re-bottling, mammoth-fur restoration, sparkle-respiking.

## Currency & units

- Primary currency: **Sparkle (★)**. Whole numbers in YAML, no symbol.
- Weights: feathers in grams, fur and metals in kilos, dragon fire in cubic centimetres (cc).
- Distances: leagues for caravan routes, fathoms for sea-deliveries, lengths-of-rainbow for sky-skiff routes.
